  • Is it safe for pregnancy?

Pregnant or lactative women should not consume bitter gourd.

  • Is it good for diabetics?

Yes.

Bittergourd contains three active ingredients such as Charantine, Vicine and Polypeptide-P

Many studies have shown that bitter gourd has a beneficial effect on reducing diabetes if it is consumed regularly at the right doses. We recommend 2 to 3 cups of Carela bittermelon tea daily. 

In a study published by the School of Biomedical Sciences, Charles Sturt University, NSW 2678, Australia says that:

"Recent scientific evaluation of this plant extracts also showed potential therapeutic benefit in diabetes and obesity related metabolic dysfunction in experimental animals and clinical studies."

https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC4306384/
